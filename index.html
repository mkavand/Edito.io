Make your content free across all devices!

Let's get to know Edito in four simple steps as Edito is all about simplicity, reliability, stability and extendability

1-CaaS

Edito Is a Content as a Service (CaaS) platform, no matter what is the context of your content like News, Article, Blog post or even Product. We want you to Rethink how you can Manage, Publish and consume Content all in and from one HUB .

2-SaaS

We are a cloud base Software as a Service (SaaS) in the meaning that you exactly pay for what you are using not a big fat cost for an expensive license which you use 10 percent of it like traditional CMSs.

3-Headless CMS

We are headless CMS in the sense that you are free to publish and expose your content through our Microservices API to whatever frontend framework you love or native devices you like.

4-Marketplace

You need a hand to shape the end user looks of your content? Not a problem we have a marketplace of gorgeous themes or even custom design facility to cover up your needs.
